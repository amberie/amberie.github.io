My name is Amber Marie Boyd. I'm currently a first year PhD student in the Department of Linguistics at Cornell University. I received my baccalaureate in Linguistics at the University of California, Berkeley in May 2023.

I'm a member of the Cornell Phonetics Lab directed by Dr. Sam Tilsen. I'm also affiliated with the Association of Nonbinary and Trans Language Researchers.

My research interests include phonetics; laboratory phonology; interactions between spoken and written modalities; documentation, revitalization, reclamation, & community work; Austronesian languages; and culture, art, life, literature, & folklore.
